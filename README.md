**Pearon is an AI companion that predicts stock and crypto price of some popular stocks and crypto currencies**

## Why making pearson ?

Most AI stock predictors show the prediction only with numbers, but they dont show why the model in those systems thinks wha it thinks, and it leads to lack in accountability, honesty, and reliability.

I wanted to build a platform where every prediction is presented with an explantion and mathematical evidences with the results. Which increase trust and reliabiliy. 

## What Pearson does ??

- It Pulls real price and history for any stock and crypto currency using 'yfinance'

- It computes exactly 19 technical indicators from the price data of different companies to gain trust and reliability.

- It pulls live news headlines about the stock or crypto currencies and then scores their sentiments using Vader

- It combines the signals into XGBoost model that will predict the price direction of next day, like UP or DOWN  and also gives a confidence percentage

- It also has an amazing and new feature, which is Bactesting. The system backtests itself on historical data using simulations and maths. 

- It has an amazig UI with graphs and all other elements required for analysis.
